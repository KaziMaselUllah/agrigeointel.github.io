About Agri GeoIntel

Agri GeoIntel is a technology startup focused on GeoAI-powered intelligence for agriculture, resilient supply chains, risk analytics, and business decision-making. We combine spatial data, artificial intelligence, simulation, and domain expertise to help stakeholders plan under uncertainty.

Our Mission

To deliver practical, science-based, and AI-enabled geospatial intelligence that supports smarter, faster, and more resilient decisions across agricultural and environmental systems.

Solutions

We develop analytical tools and decision-support systems for agriculture, markets, logistics, risk management, and emerging data-driven business applications.

🌾 Agriculture Intelligence

Crop monitoring, spatial risk assessment, production intelligence, and agricultural planning.

🛰 GeoAI Analytics

Remote sensing, GIS, machine learning, and geospatial data integration for decision support.

🚚 Supply Chain Resilience

Analytics for disruption, logistics risk, perishable food systems, and adaptive planning.

🤖 AI Decision Agents

AI-assisted workflows that help businesses interpret data, compare scenarios, and act faster.

GAIT™ Platform

GAIT™ — GeoAI Intelligence Tool — is Agri GeoIntel’s emerging platform concept for turning geospatial data, uncertainty, and business context into decision-ready intelligence.

Predictive Insights

Forecast risks, disruptions, and spatial patterns using AI and geospatial modeling.

Scenario Analysis

Compare alternative decisions under changing market, environmental, and operational conditions.

Interactive Dashboards

Translate complex data into simple visual tools for managers, researchers, and partners.

Research & Innovation

Agri GeoIntel is developing solutions at the intersection of GeoAI, agent-based modeling, agricultural data science, supply chain analytics, and decision intelligence. Our current innovation focus includes resilient perishable fruit supply chains, AI-enabled spatial analytics, and scalable decision-support tools for agricultural businesses.
